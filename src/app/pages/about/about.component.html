Our Story

Equilibrium was born from the real-life struggles of students facing stress, anxiety, and burnout in silence. Inspired by these stories, we created a platform that blends technology with empathy, giving voice and support to those who need it most.

Since our participation in the Hult Prize social entrepreneurship competition, we've refined our model with feedback from educators, psychologists, and hundreds of students.
