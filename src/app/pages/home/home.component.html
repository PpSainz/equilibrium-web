What Is equilibrium?

It is a mental health platform focused on university students. It provides immediate emotional support through an AI chatbot, self-care tools, and guided practices to reduce stress and strengthen resilience.

How It Works

Connect

Chat with the AI assistant and receive emotional guidance.

Explore

Access exercises like guided breathing or emotion tracking.

Grow

Receive personalized tips and suggestions to enhance your well-being.

Why It Matters

Almost 1 in 3 college students experience mental health issues

Many don't receive the help they need. Equilibrium fills that gap with an accessible, confidential, and empathetic solution, preventing crises and promoting emotional well-being.

Features

AI-powered emotional support chatbot

Breathing and relaxation tools

Mood and emotion tracking

Guided journaling prompts

Personalized recommendations
